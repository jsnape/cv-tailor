OverviewAt Microsoft AI, our Health team is on a mission to help millions of users better understand and proactively manage their health and wellbeing. We’re responsible for ensuring that Microsoft AI’s models and services are useful, trusted and safe across diverse customer health journeys. Our goal is to translate cutting-edge research—like MAI-DxO—into transformative products for millions of users across Copilot.com and Microsoft’s consumer ecosystem.About The RoleYou'll sit between our research team and product engineering—prototyping breakthrough features, designing evaluation frameworks, and shipping safe, trusted health AI experiences. This isn't a pure research role or a pure engineering role. It's for a builder who thrives in the messy middle where frontier AI meets real users.In this role you will;Bridge research and product: Translate cutting-edge AI research into prototypes and product opportunities. Shape roadmaps with both teams to define the future of consumer health AI.Build evaluation systems: Design frameworks and infrastructure that measure model quality, safety, and utility in healthcare contexts. Create reproducible pipelines that scale across the organization.Ship prototypes rapidly: Experiment with agent architectures, prompting techniques, and new interaction paradigms. Build demos that help teams evaluate feasibility and user value.Drive decisions with data: Interpret evaluation results and present actionable recommendations to leadership, researchers, and product teams. Advocate for evidence-based product decisions.Lead through expertise: Stay current with frontier AI research and healthcare applications.
